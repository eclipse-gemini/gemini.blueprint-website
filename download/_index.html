Latest version - 2.0.0.RELEASE

As of 2.0.0, the gemini blueprint artifacts are available directly from maven central. You may reference them in a maven pom, like so: 
        <dependency>
            <groupId>org.eclipse.gemini.blueprint</groupId>
            <artifactId>gemini-blueprint-io</artifactId>
            <version>2.0.0.RELEASE</version>
        </dependency>
        <dependency>
            <groupId>org.eclipse.gemini.blueprint</groupId>
            <artifactId>gemini-blueprint-extender</artifactId>
            <version>2.0.0.RELEASE</version>
        </dependency>
        <dependency>
            <groupId>org.eclipse.gemini.blueprint</groupId>
            <artifactId>gemini-blueprint-core</artifactId>
            <version>2.0.0.RELEASE</version>
        </dependency>

The additional mock and test dependencies are available via

        <dependency>
            <groupId>org.eclipse.gemini.blueprint</groupId>
            <artifactId>gemini-blueprint-mock</artifactId>
            <version>2.0.0.RELEASE</version>
        </dependency>
        <dependency>
            <groupId>org.eclipse.gemini.blueprint</groupId>
            <artifactId>gemini-blueprint-test</artifactId>
            <version>2.0.0.RELEASE</version>
        </dependency>
